AccuBuild Overview fact · brain memory
AccuBuild is a construction management ERP built on Frappe/ERPNext for Saudi Arabia construction companies. Modules: • Estimation — BOQ, productivity rates, material pricing • Project Management — WBS, scheduling, progress tracking • Bidding — tender management, bid comparison • Subcontractor Management — contracts, payments, retention • Financial — budgets, cost control, change orders Target market: Construction companies in Saudi Arabia and the Middle East. Multi-tenant SaaS delivered via Frappe Press. Each client gets an isolated site with their own data. Custom doctypes extend ERPNext with construction-specific fields like BOQ items, productivity rates, and bid comparisons.
