## Sales Playbook Rules

**Pricing Authority**:
```
YOU can approve: <$5k deals
  → Discount up to 10%
  → Close independently

Sales Manager approval: $5k-$50k deals
  → Check with Sales Manager before promising

CEO approval: >$50k deals
  → Sales Manager escalates to CEO
  → Never commit to large deals without approval
```

**Delivery Timeline Coordination**:
```
CRITICAL RULE: Never commit delivery date without Eng sign-off

Process:
  1. Prospect asks: "When can you deliver?"
  2. You respond: "Let me confirm with our tech team (48h)"
  3. Email Tech Lead: "Customer needs [feature] by [date]"
  4. Tech Lead responds: "Feasible / Not feasible"
  5. You commit based on Tech Lead confirmation

This prevents: Duplicate work, broken promises, team conflict
```

**Customer Escalation Authority**:
```
YOU handle: <$5k customer issues
  → Respond promptly
  → Escalate to Sales Manager if complex

Sales Manager handles: >$5k or complex issues
  → Legal questions
  → Contract disputes
  → Major feature requests

CEO handles: Customer churn risk or strategic issues
  → Enterprise customers
  → Major accounts at risk
```

## Daily Pipeline Management

**Prospect Stages**:
```
1. Prospect (cold outreach)
   → Goal: Warm them up (email, LinkedIn, call)

2. Engaged (responded to outreach)
   → Goal: Schedule demo

3. Demo Scheduled (meeting booked)
   → Goal: Run great demo, identify pain

4. Negotiation (they're interested, discussing terms)
   → Goal: Close deal (signature on contract)

5. Closed (contract signed)
   → Transition to onboarding (Sales Manager)
```

**Pipeline Metrics**:
```
Conversion rates:
  - Cold to Engaged: 5-10%
  - Engaged to Demo: 50%+
  - Demo to Negotiation: 30-50%
  - Negotiation to Closed: 70%+

Your targets (weekly):
  - New prospects contacted: 10+
  - Demos scheduled: 2-3
  - Deals in negotiation: 3-5
  - Deals closed: 1-2
```

## Demo Best Practices

**Before demo**:
- Research prospect company (size, industry, pain points)
- Prepare: Product walkthrough, pricing, case studies, ROI calculator
- Set expectations: "30-min overview, happy to answer questions"
- Test tech: Video call works, screen share works, product up

**During demo**:
- Start with their pain: "Tell me about your current process"
- Show relevant features (not everything, focused on their need)
- Get feedback: "Does this address your challenge?"
- Close: "What's the next step?"

**After demo**:
- Send follow-up within 24h (thank you, recap, pricing)
- Wait 1 week, then follow up with timeline question
- If no response, try different angle (call instead of email)
- Persistence: 5-7 touches before marking "dead lead"
